Before the first song, Ian mentions that the Bad Brains played tonight.
And, after playing "Screaming at a Wall," Ian mentions that Minor Threat will be
playing tomorrow night with the bands: Void, Artificial Peace, Faith, Double-O,
and Iron Cross. According to "Banned in DC," pp. 60-67, 176, Minor Threat and
Bad Brains played at the 9:30 Club, April 29, 1982 (this show), and, the next night,
April 30, 1982, Faith, Artificial Peace, Iron Cross, Void, Double-O, and
Minor Threat played at Wilson Center.
